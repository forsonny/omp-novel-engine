# Human Gate Rules

## Master rule

Strict human-in-the-loop is mandatory.

No downstream drafting step may run until the current gate is approved by the human.

## Gate sequence

- premise
- worldbuilding
- knowledge graph seed
- series bible
- plot/event/character/POV infrastructure
- seven-point beats
- pre-prose judges
- chapter variant choice
- post-prose quality gates
- chapter complete

## Session stop rule

`session_stop` may save state, update MCP memory/KG, queue audits, and write resumable handoff notes. It must never continue prose drafting.

## Chapter complete rule

A chapter is complete only when all mandatory gates pass and the human approves it.
